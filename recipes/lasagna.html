Lasagna

Description

This is a homemade lasagna recipe with a meaty, tomato sauce and a deliciously cheesy filling. Serve with a leafy green salad and a crusty garlic bread.

Ingredients:
  • Meat: 1/2 pound ground pork, 1/2 pound lean ground beef
  • Onion
  • Crushed tomatoes
  • Fresh herbs
  • Sugar
  • Noodles
  • Cheese
  • Spices and seasonings
  • Eggs
